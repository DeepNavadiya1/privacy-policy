Introduction

QR Code Scanner & Generator ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Storage

QR Code Scanner & Generator does not require account registration. The App stores generated QR codes, scan history, favorites, folders, custom titles, and user-created content locally on your device using local database storage (SQLite). This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Camera Permission

The camera permission is used exclusively for scanning QR codes and barcodes. Images captured during scanning are not stored, uploaded, or shared with any third party.

Local Data Storage

The App stores generated QR codes, scan history, favorites, folders, and other user-created content locally on your device. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

QR Code Content

QR code information such as URLs, text, contacts, Wi-Fi credentials, phone numbers, SMS data, email data, locations, calendar events, and other supported QR formats may be stored locally on your device when you choose to save them.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and process data in accordance with its own privacy policies.
